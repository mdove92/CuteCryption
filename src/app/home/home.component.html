Create Encrypted Messaging Templates with CuteCryption

CuteCryption makes it easy to create and customize message templates to include on your webpage. Each template provides custom keys to make your form unique to your website.




Why Use Encryption?

Encryption helps keep messages sent over the internet safe by changing the data into an unreadable format. Keep those information thieves out of your business!

Read details below to learn more about CuteCryption.

CuteCryption allows you to build a form for your website visitors to send you messages on. The only setup you need is to download Mailvelope, which is Cutecryption's decrypter of choice. Then, simply click on create a template.

How To Use Cutecryption

Step 1: Download Mailvelope

Mailvelope is Cutecryption's client of choice to decrypt your encrypted messages.

It is able to read encrypted messages sent to your inbox from within your browser. Mailvelope also is helpful in that it stores your private keys, which allows for you to never have to unsafely transfer your private key anywhere. It will ask for permission to read data.

Once you grant permission, Mailvelope will do the work of making your messages readable to you!

Step 2: Create a template

Navigate to the "Create a Template" page.

Be sure to fill in the email address at which you would like to receive encrypted messages.

Create a password that you will remember.

Mailvelope will ask you for this password when you attempt to read an encrypted message.

Click Submit, and Cutecryption will generate your html, public key, and private key for you!

Step 3: Download your keys and take your html to your site!

Copy the generated html to your clipboard.

Make sure to download your public and private keys at this point. Learn more about importing keys.

Mailvelope will need these to decrypt your messages.
